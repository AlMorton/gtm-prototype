About

This is a throwaway prototype site whose only job is to give Google Tag Manager something to attach to. Use it to validate your container setup before rolling tags out to a real site.

How to verify GTM is working

1. Preview mode

In GTM, click Preview and enter this site's URL to open Tag Assistant.

2. Watch the dataLayer

Open DevTools → Console and type dataLayer to inspect pushed events.

3. Trigger events

Click buttons and submit the contact form, then confirm tags fire in Tag Assistant.
